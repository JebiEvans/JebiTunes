Here is a tip:


Rotate your phone for actuall view
1. Click on the coloured boxes for sound.
2. All sounds have been categorized under into six groups i.e (Vox,Lead,Perc,Pad,Drum,Base)
3. Click one box at a time under the same category.
NB:Playing more than three sounds from the same category produces disorganized sounds.
4. All sounds donot exceed 21seconds.
5. To keep the sounds playing click the boxes once in a while. 6. Refreshing the page stops all sounds playing.
